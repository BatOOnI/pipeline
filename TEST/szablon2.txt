Every proper garden room starts with the base. Our builds are constructed on reinforced concrete slab foundations, not lightweight pads or cheap temporary bases. That gives the structure proper support, better long-term stability and a stronger platform for the whole build.

Our floors are raised off the slab base and ventilated. That matters. It helps protect the structure, improves durability and reduces the risk of long-term moisture-related problems. Combined with proper membranes and insulation, this creates a far more robust garden building than many prefabricated units.
